EAST WEST COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING, a place set up to train student minds at a stretch with distinctive professional gurus. EWCE, Training and Placement department takes privilege to witness its stand that students from all streams have been placed in different nooks and corners of the country. All the students' of EWCE undergo a compulsory internship in various corporate companies and research institutes in final year.

WE ARE PROUD TO HVAE OUR STUDENTS PLACED TO TOP COMPANIES

Quality guidance would be given to every student with a congeal atmosphere in order to develop them with required professional skills. EWCE, believes that students' career is the prime motto. Over the years, the institute has witnessed a good quality of academic standards that has lead to collaborate with many leading industries and these reputed organizations look at EWCE, as a place for capturing well trained professionals into their folds. Placement division at EWCE, not only exercises in placing student wings without an appropriate training. The department has an in house soft skill trainer to train as per the needs and requirements of the corporate world. The placement department organizes seminars, Guest Lectures, Workshops, Career Guidance programs, Industrial visits, Quiz, Debate and Group discussions to provide a wider exposure to student community.
